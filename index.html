International French Spell Bee Competition
Won Accolades In International French Spell Bee Competition

Students of The Millennium School, Kurukshetra win accolades in the prestigious International French Spell Bee Competition held at India International Centre, New Delhi on 16th January 2018. Yashvardhan Gupta of class VII secured third position in the intermediate level. He has been awarded a bronze medal, commendation certificate, school trophy and an attractive gift. He has also been given an opportunity to attend a five days adventure camp to be held at Rishikesh. Ansh Goel of class VII also cleared the Tie-breaker Round and got a medal and commendation certificate. Both the students marked their presence among the national and international participants from Kuwait, Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Singapore, etc. The competition was organized by Frehindi Education Private Limited, Paris. Principal Dr. A. K. Singh congratulated the winners, their French teacher and parents on this commendable achievement.

KHELO INDIA NATIONAL LEVEL SHOOTING CHAMPIONSHIP
WINS GOLD MEDAL IN KHELO INDIA NATIONAL LEVEL SHOOTING CHAMPIONSHIP

Nancy of The Millennium School, Kurukshetra has proved her mettle in Khelo India national level shooting championship 2019-20 held at Guwahati from 10 January to 22 January. She clinched the Gold medal by securing the first position in 0.177 peep sight air rifle category. Nancy scored 249.1 at national level and also won a scholarship at this esteemed platform. The enthusiastic victor had already performed phenomenally well at SGFI national shooting championship under-17, got the third position and won bronze medal in the same category. Nancy has toiled diligently to achieve this stature. Principal Dr. A.K. Singh congratulated the proud laureate and her parents on this praiseworthy achievement and wished her all the best for bright future.

SAE AWIM INDIA OLYMPICS ZONAL LEVEL COMPETITION
SAE AWIM INDIA OLYMPICS ZONAL LEVEL COMPETITION

Students of The Millennium School, Kurukshetra brought laurels to the school and Kurukshetra District by bagging the first prize in Zonal level competitions organized at JMIETI, Radaur (Yamunanagar) held on 30th October 2018 (Tuesday). The competitions were organized under the tutelage of Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) Northern India Section – ‘A World In Motion’ (AWIM) and was sponsored by Maruti Suzuki India Ltd. More than 26 teams from Kurukshetra, Yamunanagar, Ambala and Karnal participated in Jet Toy and Skimmer category. Another team of class V students also participated in Skimmer Boat Competition and got an award for “Maximum Distance Travelled”. A Poster making competition for teachers was also organized in which the Mentor teachers of The Millennium School, Kurukshetra, Mrs. Ritika and Mrs. Sween also won the first prize. The students who will be going for the National level competitions at Chitkara University, Chandigarh are Nikhil Raj, Bhavil, Shivam, Chanidhy. Principal Dr. A. K. Singh congratulated the winner students and their mentors on this spectacular achievement and wished them all the best for the national level competitions.

Parents were acquainted with the most innovative and enjoyable teaching- learning system which includes skill based teaching, innovative projects, remedial classes and performance enhancement plans.
